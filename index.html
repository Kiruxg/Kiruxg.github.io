About me

I'm Kirubel Girma

As we continue to live in these rapidly evolving times, I believe that pursuing a multi-faceted discipline is beneficial to confronting the obstacles developers may encounter throughout their journey. I am a sophomore (second year) studying at the University of California San Diego, where I am majoring in Cognitive Science with a specialization in Machine Learning and Neural Computation. My minor in design delves into UI/UX and Human-Computer Interaction.

I am currently spending time to understanding the scopes of data science and analysis and machine learning through a self-taught and learn-by-doing approach. Most importantly, I am most passionate about front-end web development and utilizing the design process to develop web applications for the user.

I typically spend my leisure time by working with the Jupyter Notebook and Sketch, learning emerging trends and technologies, staying physically active, and networking with my fellow peers. I hope to expand my expertise in my multi-disciplined learning path by exploring opportunities that can help us tackle the issues communities face with learning anything imaginable or unimaginable so that everyone can contribute toward a finer society.
